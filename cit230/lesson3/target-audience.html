Target Audience

The visitor demographic of this website will be people who lives locally in Eastern Idaho as well as travellers coming to Idaho for the National Parks like Grand Teton National Park and Yellowstone. The locals can be anything from Idaho Farmers to college students living in the area. They live in cities like Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven, and Storm Center.

Senarios

  • A family going to Yellowstone and their rest-stop is in Preston, ID.
  • An Idaho potato farmer looking to see what's the weather is like in the next week to see how he can take care of his farm.
  • A college student leaving in the morning to go to class all day long and is not sure if he needs to bring an umbrella.

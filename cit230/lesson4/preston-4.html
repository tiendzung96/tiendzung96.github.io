ALERT: Big Storm Coming to Preston

The Preston-area experienced the first of two periods of concentrated rain Tuesday night into Wednesday morning and a second is predicted Wednesday night into Thursday morning, forecasters say. Widespread rainfall estimates of 4 to 7 inches are expected through Thursday, with totals possibly reaching up to 12 inches in some isolated areas.
